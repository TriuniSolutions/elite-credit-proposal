Compliance

A2P 10DLC — SMS Compliance Setup

Before your GHL SMS can reach clients, your business needs to be registered with US carriers. I'll handle the full setup as part of this build.

What is A2P 10DLC

Why This Matters

A2P 10DLC is a mandatory US carrier requirement for any business sending automated SMS messages. Without it, your texts won't reach clients — or worse, your number gets flagged as spam. This is non-negotiable for any credit repair company doing SMS follow-ups.

What I'll Handle for You
The entire registration and compliance setup is included in the build. Here's exactly what that covers:
STEP 01
Brand Registration
Register Elite Credit Group as a verified brand inside GHL using your EIN. Includes fast-track submission for approval within 3 business days.
STEP 02
Campaign Registration
Set up your SMS campaign with approved use-case descriptions and sample messages. GHL auto-submits the campaign once brand is approved.
STEP 03
Opt-in Compliance
Set up compliant opt-in language on the intake form — unchecked consent checkbox, privacy policy link, and SMS terms. Required for carrier approval.
STEP 04
SMS Workflow Activation
Once approved, activate all SMS workflows inside GHL. Lead follow-ups, appointment reminders, and client updates all running natively.
The one-time A2P registration fee is approximately $24 paid directly by Elite Credit Group to GHL. There's also a monthly campaign fee of up to $11/mo. These are carrier fees, not part of the project cost.
